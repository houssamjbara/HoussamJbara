About the Artist

The Visionary Behind the Art

I'm Houssam Jbara, a self-taught tattoo artist and painter from Tyre, Lebanon. At a young age, I dedicated myself fully to art, and since 2019 I've been building a path through black and grey realism and surrealism in tattoos, as well as colorful and surreal explorations in painting.

My work flows from within, guided by visions and messages from a deeper place, and finds expression on skin, canvas, and digital platforms. For me, art is more than a craft; it's a way to connect with emotion, depth, and the unseen.

Each piece I create tells a story of transformation and inner vision—whether it's the permanent artistry that adorns someone's skin or the vibrant surreal worlds that come alive on canvas. My journey as a self-taught artist has been one of constant discovery, where technique serves the deeper calling to bring the invisible into form.
